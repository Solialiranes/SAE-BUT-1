Schedule of the day

Here is the schedule of the wedding's day. Schedules are subject to change slightly on the day if there are delays. Please take these times as a guide.


13H30 - Arrival of the guests at the town hall

14H00 - Civil wedding at the town hall

15H00 - Religious wedding in the church

16H00 - Procession to the Abbey

16H30 - Photo session

17h30 - Refreshments / Orchestra

If you have requested a room at the hotel (from the form), you will be guided to your room so you can put your things down during this intermission.


19H00 - Vin d'honneur

20H00 - Start of the meal

22H30 - Lantern release

23H00 - Start of the dance moment

02H00 - End of the wedding

After the end of the wedding, a small candlelight walk will take place to reach the hotel.
