Manuel and Hélène invite you to their wedding which will take place on saturday, May 21.

Come and celebrate the union of our 2 souls during our wedding in a unique, zen and relaxing setting : The Abbaye des Vaux de Cernay.

You can find the programme of the day as well as the registration form via the different parts of our site.

We met by chance in Cambodia while we were both on vacation on our side. And it was love at first sight ! After 5 years of living together, we finally said yes to wedding.

So we want to celebrate our union in a haven of peace, an old Abbey in the heart of the forest of Rambouillet. This idyllic corresponds to our desire to share a real moment of conviviality between us and those present on this wonderful day.

Thanks to the gallery you will find other images of the Abbey as well as photographs of the hotel located on the spot. There is also a selection of photos from our different trips around the world so that you can understand the atmosphere we want to bring out during this incredible moment. In addition, this gallery will be updated with the photographs taken on the wedding day.

Come dressed as you wish, we want you to feel as good as we do during this unique momentof our lives. Moreover a hotel is at your disposal if you wish to sleep on the site.

We hope to see you soon among us !

Wedding of Manuel Ribeiro and Hélène Weixin
